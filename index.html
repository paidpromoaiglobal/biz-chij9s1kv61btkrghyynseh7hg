Our Story

Rajgharana ethnic wear

Rajgharana Ethnic Wear is your trusted destination for exquisite ethnic clothing in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Nestled in the vibrant Raja Park area near Vaishno Devi Temple, our store brings together the richness of traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary style. Whether you're dressing for a grand wedding, a festive occasion, or a formal meeting, we have the perfect outfit waiting for you — stitched with care and finished to perfection.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, reflected in our perfect 5-star rating from over 243 happy customers. Our owner, fondly known as Sanju Bhai, is celebrated for his warm nature, attentive service, and expert guidance. From quick alterations done in minutes to full custom tailoring delivered within 48 hours, we go above and beyond to ensure every garment fits like it was made just for you — because it was.
