Our Story

Rajgharana ethnic wear

Rajgharana Ethnic Wear is Jaipur's beloved destination for authentic Indian ethnic clothing, nestled in the vibrant Raja Park area near Vaishno Devi Temple. With a perfect 5-star reputation built on trust, craftsmanship, and a deeply personal approach to every customer, we bring the rich textile heritage of Rajasthan to life through beautifully tailored suits, traditional wear, and home fashion pieces that celebrate Indian culture.

Owned and operated by the warm and dedicated Sanju Bhai, Rajgharana is known not just for its top-notch fabric quality and immaculate stitching, but for the care that goes into every stitch. Whether you need a wedding outfit ready in 48 hours or a quick alteration done in minutes, our team is committed to delivering perfection every single time. Customers travel from across the city — and even internationally — because they know quality and courtesy are guaranteed here.

  • Fast turnaround — full outfits ready in as little as 48 hours
  • Expert alterations done on the spot within minutes
